Company Overview

Apex Origin Global is an India-based merchant export company specializing in origin-direct sourcing of agricultural and food products from Karnataka. The company works with regional processing clusters and manufacturers to consolidate export shipments for international distributors, wholesalers and supermarket procurement teams.
