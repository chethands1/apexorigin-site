Information

Procurement FAQ

Common technical and operational questions from our international buyers.

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

For agricultural commodities like Sona Masuri Rice, our standard MOQ is a 20ft Full Container Load (FCL). For processed FMCG snacks, we accept LCL (Less than Container Load) shipments starting at 500 kg, allowing buyers to test their local market demand before scaling.

Do you provide product samples?

Yes. We provide complete, GCC-compliant product sample boxes via DHL. The buyer is only required to cover the international courier charges, which will be fully deducted from the final invoice of your first commercial order.

Can you handle dual-language labeling for the Middle East?

Absolutely. We are fully equipped to apply dual-language (English/Arabic) ingredient and nutritional stickers on all FMCG goods to ensure total compliance with GCC customs and municipality regulations.

What are your standard payment terms?

To protect both parties, we operate on standard international trade terms. We accept Irrevocable Letters of Credit (LC) at Sight from prime global banks. Alternatively, we accept Telegraphic Transfer (TT) with a 50% advance and 50% balance against scanned copies of the Bill of Lading (B/L) and export documents.
