      Logistics & Trade Terms

      We Handle Everything to Your Port

      From a shared container to a full 40-foot box — we manage sourcing, packing, port logistics, and documentation end to end.

      Understanding Your Trade Terms

      FOB
      Free On Board
      We deliver your goods to the Indian port and load them aboard the vessel. You pay ocean freight and insurance from that point onward.
      ✓ We: loading & port You: freight + insurance
      CFR
      Cost & Freight
      We pay ocean freight to your destination port. You arrange insurance and handle customs clearance at your end. Most popular for experienced importers.
      ✓ We: loading + freight You: insurance + clearance
      CIF
      Cost, Insurance & Freight
      We pay freight AND insurance to your destination port. Ideal for first-time buyers. You handle customs clearance locally. Simplest option.
      ✓ We: freight + insurance You: clearance only

      Shipping & Logistics

      LCL (Less than Container Load): your goods share a container with other exporters. Ideal for orders below 15–18 MT. Lower entry cost but slightly higher per-unit freight and longer transit due to consolidation at both ends.

      FCL (Full Container Load): you fill the entire container. Available in 20ft (~24–26 MT of rice) and 40ft (~26–28 MT). Lower per-unit freight for large orders and faster customs clearance.

      FOB (Free On Board): we deliver to the Indian port and load the goods. You pay freight and insurance from that point onward.

      CFR (Cost and Freight): we pay ocean freight to your destination port. You arrange insurance and handle customs clearance.

      CIF (Cost, Insurance and Freight): we pay both freight and insurance to your destination port. You handle clearance. CIF is the most common choice for first-time buyers.

      Yes. We work with established freight forwarding partners across all major Indian ports. Under CFR or CIF terms we manage ocean freight booking on your behalf. If you prefer your own freight forwarder, we coordinate with them directly for loading instructions and documentation.
