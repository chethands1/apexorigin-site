      Logistics & Trade Terms

      We Handle Everything to Your Port

      From a shared container to a full 40-foot box — we manage sourcing, packing, port logistics, and all documentation end to end.

      Understanding Your Trade Terms

      FOB
      Free On Board
      We deliver to the Indian port and load aboard the vessel. You pay ocean freight and insurance from that point onward.
      ✓ We: loading & port You: freight + insurance
      CFR
      Cost & Freight
      We pay ocean freight to your destination port. You arrange insurance and handle customs clearance. Most popular for experienced importers.
      ✓ We: loading + freight You: insurance + clearance
      CIF
      Cost, Insurance & Freight
      We pay freight AND insurance to your destination port. Ideal for first-time buyers. You handle customs clearance locally.
      ✓ We: freight + insurance You: clearance only

      Payment

      Bank Transfer (TT) — 50% advance at order confirmation, 50% against the Bill of Lading copy before shipment release.

      An irrevocable Letter of Credit at Sight is a bank payment instrument. Your bank guarantees payment to us once we present the correct shipping documents (BL, invoice, packing list, CoO, phytosanitary certificate). Neither party takes credit risk.

      EU imports of rice and coconut products require: phytosanitary certificate, certificate of origin, commercial invoice with HS code, packing list, and bill of lading. For retail sale, EU nutritional labelling (Regulation 1169/2011) is mandatory. Food contact packaging must comply with EU Regulation 10/2011.

      Shipping & Logistics

      We monitor all shipments after dispatch and proactively notify you of any carrier delays. In case of port congestion or force majeure, we provide updated ETAs and, where applicable, expedited re-routing options. All delays beyond our control are communicated as soon as we're informed by the carrier.

      LCL (Less than Container Load): your goods share a container with other exporters. Ideal for orders below 15–18 MT. Lower entry cost but slightly higher per-unit freight and longer transit due to consolidation at both ends.

      FCL (Full Container Load): you fill the entire container. Available in 20ft (~24–26 MT of rice) and 40ft. Lower per-unit freight for large orders and faster customs clearance.

      FOB (Free On Board): we deliver to the Indian port and load the goods. You pay freight and insurance onward.

      CFR (Cost and Freight): we pay ocean freight to your destination port. You arrange insurance and handle clearance.

      CIF (Cost, Insurance and Freight): we pay both freight and insurance to your destination port. You handle clearance — the most common choice for first-time buyers.

      Yes. We work with established freight forwarding partners across all major Indian ports. Under CFR or CIF terms we manage ocean freight booking on your behalf. If you prefer your own freight forwarder, we coordinate with them directly for loading instructions and documentation.
