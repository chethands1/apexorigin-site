Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to operational queries for international buyers.

What is the standard lead time for an FCL order?

Standard lead time is 14–21 days from the receipt of the confirmed Purchase Order and Advance Payment (or operable LC). This covers processing, grading, custom packing, pre-shipment inspection, and port clearance. Transit time is additional based on the destination port.

Do you offer private label (OEM) packaging?

Yes. We offer comprehensive private labeling for wholesale and supermarket brands. Buyers must provide print-ready artwork. Private label orders require an initial lead time extension of 3-4 weeks for bag/carton cylinder manufacturing and printing. Minimum order applies (typically 3+ FCL).

How do you handle Arabic or dual-language labeling?

For shipments destined for the GCC (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman, etc.), we apply compliant dual-language (English/Arabic) stickers featuring nutritional information, production/expiry dates, origin, and importer details as required by local municipality regulations.

How does Letter of Credit (LC) payment work with you?

We accept 100% Irrevocable, Confirmed Letter of Credit at Sight from top-tier international banks. Draft LC must be shared for our banking team's approval before issuance. Transshipment and partial shipments must be allowed. Discrepancy fees are to the buyer's account.

Can you arrange DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)?

No. We specialize in FOB, CFR, and CIF terms. Under CIF, we cover the main carriage freight and marine insurance to your destination port. Customs clearance, import duties, and inland transport in the destination country are the sole responsibility of the buyer/importer.
