      Importer FAQ

      Clear Answers for Global Buyers.

      Everything you need to know about our compliance, minimums, and documentation. Don't see your question? Contact us.

      Orders & Samples
      What are your Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ)?

      For Sona Masuri rice, our MOQ is one 20-foot Full Container Load (FCL), which holds approximately 24–26 metric tonnes depending on the bag sizes.

      For coconut products, we accommodate Less than Container Load (LCL) shipments starting at 500 kg, allowing you to test market reception before scaling.

      Can I request product samples before a commercial order?

      Yes. We send internationally compliant sample packs via DHL or FedEx. You only cover the international courier charge, which we fully deduct from your first commercial invoice.

      Do you offer Private Label (OEM) packaging?

      Absolutely. We provide full private label services for supermarket chains and distributors. This requires a minimum order of 3 FCL and adds 3–4 weeks to the initial lead time for packaging production.

      Quality & Compliance
      Are your products Halal certified?

      Our agricultural products are naturally Halal. Formal Halal certification by an accredited Indian Islamic body (often required for GCC/ASEAN retail) can be supplied with your documents upon request.

      Can I appoint a third-party inspector (SGS/Bureau Veritas)?

      Yes, we welcome pre-shipment inspections. You may appoint any recognized agency to inspect the goods at our facility or the port before container stuffing. We provide full cooperation and access.

      Payment & Documentation
      What are your exact payment terms?

      We operate on two primary structures:

      • LC at Sight: Irrevocable Letter of Credit from a top-tier bank (recommended for new buyers).
      • Telegraphic Transfer (TT): 50% advance against Pro-forma Invoice, 50% balance against the scanned copy of the Bill of Lading.
      Do you provide Preferential Certificates of Origin?

      Yes. For countries with FTAs with India (e.g., ASEAN, UAE CEPA, SAFTA), we issue the necessary preferential forms (Form A, Form AI, etc.) to ensure you benefit from reduced import tariffs.

      Regional Import Requirements
      Can you meet GCC Arabic labelling requirements?

      Yes. We routinely print and apply dual-language (English/Arabic) labels containing all mandatory GCC fields: ingredients, nutritional facts, origin, and importer details.

      What is required for EU & UK imports?

      We provide the mandatory Phytosanitary Certificate, standard CoO, and commercial documents. We also ensure packaging complies with EU Regulation 1169/2011 for food information. We advise using a licensed customs broker at your destination port.

      What is required for US imports (FDA)?

      The importer must handle FDA Prior Notice and be a registered Importer of Record. We supply the required HTS codes (e.g., 1006.30 for milled rice), Phyto, and commercial documents. FDA may conduct randomized testing upon arrival.
