      Common Questions

      Questions Buyers Ask Us

      Simple answers to the things every importer wants to know before placing their first order.

      What is the smallest order I can place?

      For Sona Masuri rice, the minimum is one 20-foot full container (FCL). For coconut products, we accept smaller shared containers (LCL) starting from just 500 kg — perfect for testing your local market before scaling up.

      Can I get samples before ordering?

      Yes. We send complete, GCC-compliant sample boxes by DHL. You only pay the courier cost, and we will deduct it fully from your first commercial order invoice.

      Do you do Arabic labelling for the Middle East?

      Absolutely. We apply dual-language English and Arabic stickers with full ingredient and nutrition information on all relevant products — ready for GCC customs clearance without any hassle on your end.

      What payment methods do you accept?

      We work with an irrevocable Letter of Credit (LC at sight) from any major international bank — the safest option for both sides. We also accept bank transfer (TT) with 50% paid upfront and the remaining 50% when we send you the shipping documents.

      How long does shipping take?

      Gulf countries (UAE, Saudi Arabia): 10–14 days. Southeast Asia: 14–20 days. UK, Europe, USA: 25–35 days. We will give you an exact estimate once we know your destination port.
